Key Findings

Reading Patterns

Group 1 reads quickly and smoothly, with minimal rereading. Group 2 reads at a medium pace but frequently goes back to reread sections. Group 3 reads slowly and spends significant time rereading previous text.

Comprehension Outcomes

All three groups achieved similar, high comprehension scores, showing that different reading styles can lead to the same successful understanding.

Overall Insights

Reading quickly does not mean worse comprehension, and reading slowly does not mean better comprehension. The most efficient readers (Group 1) achieved high comprehension in the shortest time.
